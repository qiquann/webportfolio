Why TikTok matters to me

TikTok is more than short videos. It is a fast moving platform where trends, humor, and storytelling come together in creative ways. As a public relations student, I enjoy seeing how creators capture attention and communicate messages in just a few seconds.

As a Singaporean, I also appreciate seeing global conversations include Asian voices, especially when moments of cultural identity go viral.

What I love about TikTok

  1. Creative expression
    • Short form storytelling
    • Editing and sound trends
    • Authentic humor
  2. Community building
  3. Fast cultural conversations
